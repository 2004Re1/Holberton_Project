Bob and Dmitri

Bobby is stuck in a world full of filthy monsters and he probably can't beat them alone.
But luckily he is not alone, his pal Dmitri has got him covered. How long can you stay alive
with ya boy Dmitri ?


This is an endless top down shooter game with growing difficulty and upgrades.
This game was originally made for Brackeys game jam 2022.2 in one week

Gameplay

    Controls


  • WASD - Move
  • Mouse - Aim
  • LMB - Shoot
  • Space - Activate Dmitri ( when available)
